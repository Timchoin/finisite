PINK & BLUE

Pink/Blue tries to connect a practical, more conservative world, in which we grew up, and the struggling urge to poetically articulate ourselves within this society. This confrontation result in a shift of moralities, motivations, and it questions usual symbols. Blue is the color that is given to boys by birth. This color will dictate fixed rules, and the inseparable human pink will have to be suppressed.
